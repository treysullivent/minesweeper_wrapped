To play our Minesweeper game:

Use Q & E to move the selected block along the Z axis
Use W & S to move the selected block along the Y axis
Use A & D to move the selected block along the X axis

Use the arrow keys to rotate the cube around (The cube can also be moved around by clicking on the upper, lower, right, and left sides of the screens)

Press F to flag a block 

Press Enter to 'Mine' A block

Try to clear the whole cube without selecting a bomb! There are 4 to find total.
